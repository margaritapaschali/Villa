The Villa

Nestled within a 4-acre olive grove, Inner Glow Villa is a flexible hideaway of two connected homes that can be enjoyed as one spacious property or split into two independent houses. With 7 bedrooms, 5 bathrooms, 1 WC, 2 kitchens and 2 living rooms, it comfortably hosts families, groups of friends or retreat participants.

Outside, the olive trees set a tranquil scene for long lunches and slow evenings. Swim in the pool, fire up the BBQ and make memories across the generous outdoor spaces.
