The Villa

Nestled within a 4-acre olive grove, Inner Glow Villa is a unique retreat designed to offer both comfort and flexibility. The property consists of two connected houses, making it ideal either as one spacious villa or as two independent homes. With 7 bedrooms, 5 bathrooms, 1 WC, 2 kitchens, and 2 living rooms, the villa can accommodate families, groups of friends, or retreat participants with ease.

The outdoor area invites you to unwind and connect with nature: enjoy the swimming pool, share meals by the BBQ, or simply relax surrounded by the serenity of olive trees. Whether you are seeking a holiday escape or a place to host meaningful gatherings, Inner Glow Villa is the perfect setting.
