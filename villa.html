The Villa

Nestled within a 4-acre olive grove, Inner Glow Villa is a flexible hideaway of two connected homes that can be enjoyed as one spacious property or as two independent houses. With 7 bedrooms, 5 bathrooms, 1 WC, 2 kitchens and 2 living rooms, it is ideal for families, groups of friends or retreat gatherings.

Outdoors you’ll find a swimming pool, a BBQ corner and generous terraces surrounded by olive trees — the perfect backdrop for long summer days and starry nights.
